Local-first · macOS

Speak. It types.
Nothing leaves your Mac.

Whispy is a private voice-dictation utility for macOS. Hold the Fn key, talk, and release — your words land in whatever app you're using. All transcription runs locally with faster-whisper. No cloud, no account, no telemetry.

Why Whispy

100% local

Audio is transcribed on-device with Whisper. Your voice never touches a server.

One key, anywhere

Hold Fn to record, release to insert text into any focused field — terminal, browser, editor.

Live waveform

A subtle on-screen waveform confirms Whispy is listening, then fades away.

Multilingual

Defaults to French and handles many languages through the Whisper model you choose.

Lives in the menu bar

Runs quietly as a background daemon. No window to manage, no dock clutter.

Tunable models

Trade speed for accuracy by picking a Whisper model size that fits your Mac.

How it works

  1. 1

    Hold Fn

    A soft chime confirms Whispy is recording.

  2. 2

    Speak

    The waveform reacts to your voice in real time.

  3. 3

    Release

    Whispy transcribes locally and types the text into the active field.

Your voice stays yours

Everything happens on your machine. There is no cloud transcription, no account, and no telemetry. Whispy uses local Whisper inference, so zero data leaves your computer — by design, not by promise.
